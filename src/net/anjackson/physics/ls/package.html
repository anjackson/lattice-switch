Potential Projects

This suite of programs could be used to study the following systems:

  • Trace the AB2 - 2FCC boundary in (volfrac,alpha) space. Eldridge et al 1993.
  • Trace the CsCl - FCC line, coming down from pure FCC in alpha.
  • Once at CsCl cross-over, check effect of polydispersity (de-stabilises CsCl?)
  • Hard-sphere solid-fluid simulation direct phase switch using displacement scaling. (PRL?)
